Objective:
The objective of this project is to analyze purchase data from an e-learning platform to understand learner behavior, sales trends, and popular course categories using MySQL.

Key Insights:

Data Science courses generated the highest revenue among all categories.

SQL and Python-related courses were the most frequently purchased.

Some learners purchased courses from multiple categories, indicating cross-domain learning interest.

A few courses had no purchases, highlighting areas for marketing improvement.

Learners from India and USA contributed significantly to total revenue.

Recommendations:

Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing categories like Data Science and Business.

Promote less popular courses through discounts or bundled offers.

Encourage cross-category learning by recommending related courses to learners.

Analyze regional demand to introduce localized course offerings.

Conclusion:
The SQL-based analysis provides actionable insights for improving sales strategy, course offerings, and learner engagement on the platform.
